About Me

With over 3 years of hands-on experience in hardware design, I specialize in taking ideas from initial concept all the way to lab validation. My work spans RF power amplifiers, UHF filters, and precision analog circuits—blending robust design principles with real-world testing.

I focus on building reliable, production-ready hardware  and  I’m driven by  and practical results.

In the lab, I’ve validated and debugged systems using tools like vector network analyzers, oscilloscopes, and spectrum analyzers. Whether I’m designing, simulating, or testing, I bring a detail-oriented, hands-on mindset to every project.

Why Put Your Trust in Me?

I may be early in my career, but I bring a strong hands-on foundation and a commitment to doing things right. I’ve worked across the full hardware development cycle—from schematic to simulation to lab validation—on projects that demand precision and performance.

I’m quick to learn, detail-focused, and not afraid to dive deep into debugging, design challenges, or compliance requirements, I bring a practical, problem-solving mindset that keeps progress moving.

If you're looking for someone who's grounded, technically sharp, and always ready to learn—I'm your guy.
